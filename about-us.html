About Little Angels' School

Nurturing Excellence Since 1981

Our Core Philosophy


Established in 1981, Little Angels' School (LAS) began with a vision to transform the educational landscape of Nepal. What started as a small community initiative has now grown into one of the country’s most prestigious institutions, sprawling across 350 ropanis of lush, green landscape in Hattiban.


Our motto, "Wisdom is Divine," serves as the foundation of our teaching methodology. We don't just focus on rote learning; we encourage our students to question, innovate, and lead. Over the past four decades, we have nurtured thousands of graduates who are now making significant impacts in medicine, engineering, arts, and entrepreneurship globally.

  • Vision: To be a global center of excellence in education, fostering a community of lifelong learners.
  • Mission: To provide a supportive and stimulating environment where students explore their full potential and develop into responsible global citizens.
  • Core Values: We instill Integrity in action, Resilience in the face of challenges, Compassion for others, and a relentless pursuit of Excellence.
